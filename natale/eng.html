🎅 Da List of “Christmas” Things

  • 🐶 A pet (real or fake) dressed up for Christmas thinkin’, “Why me?”

  • 💫 Somethin’ with WAY too much glitter – if ya sparkle for three days, ya done good

  • 📝 A sign that you can pretend says “Merry Christmas” – even if it says somethin’ else!
  • 🌵 Somethin’ that should NOT be decorated for Christmas – like, I dunno, your toothbrush?
  • 🧙 A DIY elf – like when you got creative back in Nonna’s kitchen! ✂️
  • 🖼️ A decoration way too serious for Christmas – like da Mona Lisa wearin’ a Santa hat?
  • 🦌 An improvised “reindeer” – your dog with antlers made from paper, that’ll do!
  • 🤔 An object that makes ya go “What’s this got to do with Christmas?” – just take da pic
  • ☹️ Somethin’ that looks like “The Grinch before coffee” ☕
  • 📜 A “creative” Christmas card – even on a paper towel, it counts!
  • 🛋️ A chair or sofa “dressed up” for Christmas – lookin’ ready for a runway! 💃
  • 📺 Somethin’ that *claims* to be Christmas-y... but it’s totally not
  • 🍅 Somethin’ red and white that’s *tryin’ its best* – like a bottle of ketchup
  • 🔔 Somethin’ that “sounds like Christmas” but isn’t – get creative, make some noise!
  • 🤷 A decoration so bizarre, it needs a couple explanations
  • 📦 Somethin’ that “wants” to be a gift – even if it’s just an empty box with a bow
